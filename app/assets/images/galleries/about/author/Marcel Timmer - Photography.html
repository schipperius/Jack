Photography

This page shows some of the photographs I have taken. Some situations are created purposively, sometimes I've just run into them. I made the photographs using either a Fuji Finepix A303, Kodak 7330 or Panasonic DMC-LS75. Just some simple digital compact cameras. You are free to use the pictures, in anyway you want to. Most have a resolution of 2048 times 1536 pixels, which should be sufficient to use them as desktop wallpapers, at least for most of you.

Extrema Outdoor 2008

A party again. This time we went to Extrema Outdoor 2008. Although the picture suggests differently the weather was pretty good!

Extrema Outdoor 2008 at AQUABest, Eindhoven, the Netherlands.

Holiday pictures Veules-les-roses, Normandie, France.

Once more we returned to Normandy. In the little town of Veules-les-roses we found a great camp site. Not far from the beach, it was the perfect location to wonder around on the beach every now and then.

Dark clouds getting nearer the clifs at Veules-les-roses.

Holiday pictures Barneville-Carteret, Normandie, France.

During this years summer holiday we returned to Barneville-Carteret, again. However, we also visited Saint-Malo in Bretagne, France.

Picture of Suzanne taken by mounting the camera to a three meter long rod. The rotational blur is purely accidental.

A house along the coast in Bretagne.

Holiday pictures Denmark/Gronninghoved

This year we didn't celebrate Christmas at home. Together with some friends we went to a nice holiday cottage in Denmark. This picture was made during a short hike throughout the neighboring forest.

Picture from a holiday in Denmark/Gronninghoved, showing Femke Gijsbers and Suzanne Pietersma.

Holiday pictures Normandie, France.

During the summer holiday we returned to Normandie, France. Just like last year we were very lucky with the weather. Though, the subtle presence of clouds has given me beautiful pictures. Fortunately this time most of the panoramas succeeded quite well as you can observe for yourself.

Suzanne, Dorien Visser, Jasper Pietersma and an anonymous fisherman.

Suzanne sitting at a typical seaside cliff in Normandie.

A view of a tree bottom up.

Suzanne Pietersma standing at a typical seaside cliff in Normandie during sunset.

Panoramic picture of the harbor in Barneville-Carteret.

Holiday pictures Katwijk aan zee

During the last weekend of October 2005, me and Suzanne went a weekend to Katwijk aan zee. I had the opportunity to quickly make a series of pictures of the coastline. Again I used the free photo stitch program developed by Canon. The lighting was rather bad, so I tried to fix the differences in light with some editing. About the results you can judge for yourself.

A panoramic picture of the Dutch coastline from The Hague to Katwijk aan Zee.

Holiday pictures France/Normandie/Port-Bail

In August 2005 we drove to Port-Bail for two weeks. Not having any experience in driving such large distances we were surprised by how near France actually is. We made an extremely large number of pictures. I've put some of the panoramas I made during the vacation down here. You're wondering how i did it? I used the free photo stitch program developed by Canon. And sorry, I can't provide you with it.

Panoramic picture of the beach at Bologne Sur Mer, France.
